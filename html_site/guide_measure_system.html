Measures and landmarks

Added in ManuelbastioniLAB 1.2

The measures currently supported in the measure system are 33 "classic" dimensions, as used in the most of anthropometric studies and researches.

The dimensions are measured using the following landmarks:

  • 1 Head-width_X. From the iliocristale to crotch, projected on Z axis
  • 2 Head-length. From the glabella to back of the head.
  • 3 Head-height_Z. From the highest point on the skull to the inferior point of the chin, projected on Z axis.
  • 4 Neck-height_Z. From the inferior point of the chin to the acromion, projected on Z axis.
  • 5 Neck-girth. The circumference is measured in the middle of the neck height, on a plane perpendicular to neck axis.
  • 6 Shoulders-width. The biacromial distance between the right and left acromion.
  • 7 Upperarm-axillary-girth. The circumference of upperarm, at level of armpit, measured on a plane perpendicular to upperarm axis.
  • 8 Upperarm-length. The distance between the acromion and radiale.
  • 9 Forearm-length. The distance between the radiale and stylion.
  • 10 Elbow-girth. The circumference measured at level of radiale, with straight arms.
  • 11 Wrist-girth. The circumference measured at level of stylion
  • 12 Hands-length. The maximum distance between the stylion and the top of fingers.
  • 13 Hands-width. The maximum distance between metacarpale II and metacarpale V
  • 14 Torso-height_Z. From the acromion to the iliocristale, projected on Z axis
  • 15 Buttock-height_Z. From the iliocristale to the acromion
  • 16 Chest-width_X. Maximum distance along the X axis, at the level of nipple. It doesn't include breast mass.
  • 17 Chest-depth_Y. Distance between nipple and back, projected on the Y axis.
  • 18 Chest-girth. Maximum circumference of chest.
  • 19 Waist-girth. Circumference at level of omphalion.
  • 20 Buttock-depth_Y. Maximum distance between front and back buttock, projected on Y axis.
  • 21 Buttock-width_X. Maximum width of buttock, projected on X axis.
  • 22 Buttock-girth. Circumference of buttock in the middle distance between iliocristale and crotch.
  • 23 Upperleg-length. Distance between the crotch and the center of patella, projected on the long axis of upperleg.
  • 24 Lowerleg-length. Distance between the center of patella and the lateral malleous, projected on the long axis of lowerleg.
  • 25 Upperleg-top-girth. Circumference of upperleg at level of end of buttock.
  • 26 upperleg-bottom-girth. Circumference of upperleg just before the knee.
  • 27 lowerleg-calf-girth. Circumference of lowerleg at level of calf.
  • 28 Lowerleg-bottom-girth. Circumference of lowerleg at level of ankle (before the malleolus)
  • 29 Feet-width. Maximum width of foot.
  • 30 Feet-length. Maximum length of foot, from back of heel to the longer toe.
  • 31 Feet-height_Z. Distance between the ground plane and the lateral malleous.
  • 32 Feet-heel-width. Maximum distance between the inner and extrnal side of the heel.
  • 33 Height_Z. Distance between the ground and the top of the head, projected on Z axis. It's a read only value, obtained as sum the sum of head-height_Z, neck-height_Z, torso-height_Z, buttock-height_Z, upperleg-length, lowerleg-length (assuming the legs measures were taken with heels togheter) and feet height_Z.

In ManuelbastioniLAB version 1.2, the measures are taken using the most probable lankmark position, not the effective position. For example the hand length is always computed considering the tip of middle finger.

If a particular character has a different proportion, for example the index finger longer than the middle finger, the measure can be unaccurate.
