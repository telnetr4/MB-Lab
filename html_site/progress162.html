Development tasks progress

Updated: 19 October 2018

To trace a clear outline of the development and ETA, the list below contains the remaining tasks to complete the release of ManuelbastioniLAB 1.6.2. The page is updated very often.

Tasks in progress for lab 1.6.2

The new base models planned for ManuelbastioniLAB 1.7.0 will be gradually released in versions 1.6.2-1.6.9. They will be included as experimental feature, since the completion of the related libraries (morphs, textures and other data) and the GUI are planned for 1.7.0. See this article for further details. Obviously, each 1.6.x will also include regular improvements and bugfixes.
