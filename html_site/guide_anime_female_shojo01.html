Anime girl style shojo01 (F_AN01.68)

F_AN01.68 is the first prototype for non human (NHU) character in the lab. It has requested 68 revisions to reach this status in ManuelbastioniLAB 1.4.0.

The topology was improved and optimized in ManuelbastioniLAB 1.1.0. See the release log 1.1.0 for more info: Release log

It includes some advanced details, for example the geometry for the NPR reflections. Teeth are completely different from the human model, since they must be rendered often as unique piece, but with a detailed line between upper and lower teeth.
