Shapekeys conversion table: names mapping from lab 1.5.0 to lab 1.6.0

08 November 2017

The expression system of ManuelbastioniLAB 1.6.0 is based on a new library of morphings. The old shapekeys of lab 1.5 are renamed (and remodelled from scratch) in order to have autoexplicative names. New advanced shapekeys are added for complex and realistic effects, as deglution and breathing. The new library also includes a complete set of phonemes (it was preferred phonemes instead of visemes, since the latter are just a subset of the former and can be easily extracted if needed). Also the new naming system permits to exchange expressions between lab anime and humans.

Since there are various animation systems based on the ManuelbastioniLAB 1.5 that need to migrate to lab 1.6, here is the conversion table that maps the shapekeys 1.5 to 1.6.
