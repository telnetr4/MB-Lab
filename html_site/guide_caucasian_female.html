Caucasian female (F_CA01.150)

The Caucasian female model F_AC01.150, modelled by Manuel Bastioni, is included in ManuelbastioniLab 1.5.0 and has required 150 steps to reach the current status. It is designed in the aim to find the best balance between human readable topology, animation topology and anatomy details.

The high quality topology includes all the main muscles and tendons, with an advanced mesh for the face, well tested for expressions. The models provides teeth and detailed eyes with separated cornea. The eyelashes are included as geometry, so they can be exported from Blender without the problem of particle conversion.

The model represents an average Caucasian woman with idealistic proportions and age between 30-33 years old.
