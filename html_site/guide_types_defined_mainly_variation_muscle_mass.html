Types defined mainly by the variation of muscle and mass percentages

These types are determined by the amount of body mass and the percentage of muscles.

For example a high amount of mass with a very low percentage of muscles defines the extreme overweight type, while a high amount of mass with high muscles percentage defines the body-builder.

On the contrary a small mass with high percentage of muscles defines an athletic, nimble and agile body, while the same mass with a low amount of muscles defines an underweight type.

The library of ManuelbastioniLAB includes the extreme combinations (body-builder, extreme overweight and extreme underweight) and some more common intermediate forms.

These are:

  1. Athletic
  2. Bodybuilder
  3. Extreme overweight
  4. Extreme underweight

These types preserve enough the bone structure and proportions, with minimal changes to face, related to the mass.
